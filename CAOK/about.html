About Us

Building a brighter future for Kenya's children

Our Story

Child Aid Organization Kenya (CAOK) was established with a vision to transform the lives of disadvantaged children through education and community development. For over two decades, we have been working tirelessly to create sustainable change in communities across Kenya.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable growth and development in schools while creating better futures for children in developing countries through education, innovation, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of transparency and accountability in all our operations.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions to address complex educational challenges.

Collaboration

We work closely with communities, governments, and partners to achieve lasting impact.

Sustainability

We develop programs that create long-term positive change in communities.
